Welcome to the Age of Revolution...

Recently, IKEA has not been on the top... The IKEA Mom Finder is about to change shopping forever! According to an internal survey, almost every single person that ever went to an IKEA, got lost there. Many of them children. This often leads to panic, crying and sadness. However, with our new product, this is about to change. Kids will finally have limitless access to their mothers when they are lost in IKEA.





What does the Mom Finder have to offer?

Well...









A Mushy Button with Deep Push:


Large and easily accessible by panicked children.









A Tracker to always keep track of your Mom:


Tiny, unnoticeable, comfortable to wear.









Front USB 3.2 Type C Ports:


Allows for very quick charge, and universal compatibility at blazing speeds.









A High Gain Antenna:


Allows for high definition data to be transmitted no matter where the mother is situated within IKEA.









High Quality Speakers:


Provides an easy, direct communication line between the mother and child.









A 4K Display Screen:


Provides high resolution visual tracking capability to the Mom Finder.









Wi-Fi Support:


Auto-connects to IKEA public Wi-Fi, to double communication security in case of thick concrete walls.









A SIM Card Slot:


Initiate Emergency calls and messages, via telecommunication technology.









Bright LEDs:


Unlike competing products, ours contain bright LEDs, which allows the user to distinguish functionality.









A Quality Clip on Tracker:


High qulity metalic clip allows for clipping onto items for up to 30°.









Quality Materials:


Our materials have been carefully hand-picked to provide the best experience, feel and grip.
